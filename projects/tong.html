Tong

Hiu Yan CHAN
"Tong", 2022
3D model sculpted in Blender, 3D-printed in PLA material, painted with acrylics, 9cm x 3cm x 8cm

This piece was 3D modelled after my friend's dog, Tong. She gave me roughly-taken reference pictures of her dog, which I studied to replicate. After modelling, I 3D-printed it and then painted it with acrylics according to Tong's fur patterns.
