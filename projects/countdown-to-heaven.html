Countdown to Heaven

Grace CHAN
"Countdown to Heaven", 2018
Wire sculpture machine

In honor of Caleb Haven Martin (1999 - 2018) our class president and friend.

"Countdown to Heaven" is a wire sculpture with mechanical parts. The sculpture resembles the shape of a coffin. In its center is a clock with a movable arm. By moving the arm, a dove above the coffin flies up and down, up and down.
