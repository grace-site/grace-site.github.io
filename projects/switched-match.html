Switched match

Hiu Yan CHAN
"Switched Match", 2018
Web game programmed with html, css, javascript

A memory matching game, except after you flipped two cards over, they switch positions.
Click here to play now.

Available in single and double player modes. Three levels of difficulties. Score is calculated based on the time and number of moves used to complete the game. When in double player mode, the player who makes a match gets to go again.
