Corprio App Icons, 2023
Vector app icons designed in Inkscape

I designed this set of app icons for the Corprio app suite. To start off, I wanted there to be a consistent theme tying them all together. Upon studying the Corprio logo, I wanted to replicate the thin, sleek lines as the uniting element. Each icon must be simple yet unique enough in shape and color to be easily distinguished from the others, and representative of its application sector.

For each icon, I made two versions to accomodate for versatility in the web environment. One with a colored background, and another with a transparent background.

The colored-background icons were exported as a square, so that the rounded corners and drop shadows may be later added by css in the browser for the best user experience and web consistency.

Besides designing the app icons, I also transformed the UI/UX experience by reimagining what I considered as a better user experience on both large screens and mobile devices. I stuck to simple, and conventional placement of navigational buttons to provide the most intuitive flow possible. I also switched up the colors and styles for a more coherent, modern look.
