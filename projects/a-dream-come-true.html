Hiu Yan CHAN
"A Dream Come True", 2020
A short film with AR motion tracking, made in Cinema4D.

As a young girl falls asleep in the library, the fairytale world wakes up.

Motion-tracking was used to detect camera movement and object boundaries. I then 3D modelled books and shelf to receive shadows of the virtual characters.

I used pre-made models and rigged them on Mixamo. Using some animation moves from Mixamo, I combined and adjusted them for the desired motions.
